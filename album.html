What is this?

A convenient and easy-to-use Spotify to MP3 converter for downloading music from Spotify. You can quickly download individual tracks, albums, or playlists (Spotify playlist downloader) by simply pasting the link. Full album or playlist ZIP downloads are supported, including MP3 format with high-quality audio. Downloaded files include metadata such as cover art, artist names, and release dates, making it easy to manage your music library. This Spotify to MP3 converter works for personal use.

Important: Free sources are used to search for music. We do not download directly from Spotify and do not store music files on our servers.

How to use the SpotSave website?
1
Copy the song or playlist URL

Open the Spotify page or app with a track or playlist that you want to save. Copy its link (three dots->share->copy link).

2
Paste the URL into the input field on SpotSave

Paste the URL onto the input field at the top of the page.

3
Click the download button

Click on the right-side button to run the downloading process

4
Download a MP3 file

The tool will show you the download links. (with ZIP download). Just click on desired song's button & save it.
